How do I list my property on the website?

To list your property, simply create an account by signing up on our website. Once you're registered, navigate to the "List Your Property" section, fill in the required details, upload photos, and submit your listing for approval. Our team will review it, and it will be live within 24-48 hours.

Some answers.

Is there a fee to list my property?

Listing a property on our website is free for basic listings. However, we offer premium listing options that include better visibility, featured placement, and additional promotional services for a fee.

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a property viewing by clicking on the "Request a Viewing" button on the property listing page. Choose a suitable date and time, and the property owner or agent will confirm the appointment.

Can I contact the property owner or agent directly?

Yes, you can contact the property owner or agent directly via the contact information provided in the listing. You can also use the "Send Message" button on the listing page to inquire about the property.

How do I search for properties within my budget?

To search for properties within your budget, use the search bar on the homepage and filter by price range. You can also refine your search by location, property type, number of bedrooms, and other criteria.

What should I do if I find a listing that seems fraudulent?

If you come across a listing that seems suspicious or fraudulent, please report it immediately by clicking the "Report Listing" button on the listing page. Our team will investigate and take necessary action to ensure the safety of all users.

How do I edit or remove my property listing?

You can edit or remove your property listing by logging into your account, going to the "My Listings" section, and selecting the property you wish to update. You can make changes or remove the listing from there.

How can I find real estate agents in my area?

You can find real estate agents by visiting our "Find an Agent" page. Enter your location or the area you're interested in, and we’ll show you a list of qualified agents with their contact information and client reviews.

What kind of properties can I list?

You can list all types of properties, including residential homes, apartments, commercial spaces, land, and vacation rentals. We cater to both buyers and renters.

Can I save listings to view later?

Yes, you can save your favorite listings by clicking the "Save Listing" button. These will be stored in your account under "Saved Properties" for easy access anytime.
